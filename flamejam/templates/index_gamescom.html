Welcome to the Game Jam!

When? Wed. 17th of August 12PM - Fri. 19th of August 2PM


Where? Cologne, Germany / Conference Center East gamescom (1000qm2)


Awardshow? Fri. 19th of August 7PM in Hall 10.1 on the Teamspeak Stage


The InnoGames Jam (#IGJAM16) is the first major game jam at gamescom sponsored through the Intel Diversity Fund. The jam will have 200 developers from 27 nations including Kenya, South Korea and Indonesia creating games in 48h and the winners get a free showcase spot at the awarded Indie Arena Booth in hall 10.1. With this event we want to show how colorful the game industry is and bring people together from all countries, all genders, beliefs etc., creating games and making this world a smaller planet.
